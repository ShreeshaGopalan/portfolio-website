About Me

Greetings! I’m Shreesha, navigating the dynamic world of Web design, where every element serves a purpose. With a strong focus on user experience and a knack for transforming ideas into visually intuitive interfaces, I approach each project with a passion for crafting something impactful.

My design journey includes an enriching internship at Open Weaver, where I worked on creating user-friendly templates and exciting client projects. Prior to that, I spent two years at Scribbles, designing functional and stylish school uniforms, which helped me develop a keen sense of how aesthetics and practicality can come together to meet user needs.

I work on HTML, CSS, Figma, and Illustrator, blending design and functionality to deliver seamless user experiences. Constantly learning and evolving, I aim to stay ahead of design trends while creating work that’s both timeless and innovative.
